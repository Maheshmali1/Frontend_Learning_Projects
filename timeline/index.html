Time-Line for Google Company evolution

  1. 1998 - Incorporation as Google
  2. 1999 - First office in Menlo Park
  3. 2004 - Initial public offering (IPO)
  4. 2005 - Google Maps
  5. 2006 - Acquisition of YouTube
  6. 2007 - Android launch
  7. 2008 - Google Chrome
  8. 2015 - Restructuring as Alphabet Inc
  9. 2016 - Google Assistant
  10. 2018 - Google Duplex
  11. 2020 - Google Meet
  12. 2023 - Google Bard
